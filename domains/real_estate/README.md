# Real Estate Domain – Business Analyst Guide

## 1. What a Business Analyst typically works on in this domain
- Portfolio performance across properties and regions.
- Leasing funnel analytics and occupancy management.
- Revenue optimization for rents and ancillary services.
- Property operations and maintenance efficiency.

## 2. Common business problems
- High vacancy rates or slow lease‑up.
- Rent concessions reducing profitability.
- Maintenance backlog impacting tenant satisfaction.
- Underperforming assets in specific markets.

## 3. Common KPIs and metrics
- Occupancy rate, lease‑up conversion rate.
- Effective rent, NOI (Net Operating Income).
- Turnover rate, average days vacant.
- Maintenance response time, tenant satisfaction.

## 4. Typical data sources
- Property management systems (PMS).
- Leasing and CRM platforms.
- Accounting and rent roll reports.
- Work order and maintenance systems.

## 5. How this domain uses the standard BA workflow
- **Problem framing:** define occupancy or revenue objective.
- **Data collection:** consolidate leasing, rent, and ops data.
- **Cleaning:** align property and unit IDs; normalize lease terms.
- **Analysis:** vacancy drivers, rent elasticity, market comparisons.
- **Insights:** quantify revenue impact and operational improvements.
- **Communication:** deliver recommendations to asset and property managers.
