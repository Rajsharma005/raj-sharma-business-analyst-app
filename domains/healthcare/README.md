# Healthcare Domain – Business Analyst Guide

## 1. What a Business Analyst typically works on in this domain
- Patient journey analytics (scheduling → visit → follow‑up).
- Operational efficiency for clinics, hospitals, or providers.
- Revenue cycle and claims processing performance.
- Quality, compliance, and care outcomes reporting.

## 2. Common business problems
- Long wait times or appointment no‑shows.
- Denied claims or slow reimbursement cycles.
- Under‑utilization of services or capacity constraints.
- Variation in care quality or outcomes across facilities.

## 3. Common KPIs and metrics
- Appointment utilization rate, no‑show rate.
- Claims denial rate, days in accounts receivable.
- Patient satisfaction (HCAHPS/CSAT), readmission rate.
- Average length of stay, throughput.

## 4. Typical data sources
- EHR/EMR systems and scheduling platforms.
- Claims and billing systems.
- Patient experience surveys.
- Operational dashboards and staffing systems.

## 5. How this domain uses the standard BA workflow
- **Problem framing:** define patient flow or revenue cycle objective.
- **Data collection:** gather scheduling, clinical, and billing data.
- **Cleaning:** de‑identify where required, align patient IDs, standardize codes.
- **Analysis:** capacity utilization, claims cycle analysis, outcome trends.
- **Insights:** quantify bottlenecks and compliance risks.
- **Communication:** deliver recommendations to clinical and ops leadership.
