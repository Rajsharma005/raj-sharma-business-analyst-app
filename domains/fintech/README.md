# Fintech Domain – Business Analyst Guide

## 1. What a Business Analyst typically works on in this domain
- User acquisition, activation, and retention for digital products.
- Payment flows, wallet usage, and transaction success rates.
- Pricing, monetization, and unit economics for growth.
- Fraud and risk controls balanced with user experience.

## 2. Common business problems
- Low activation after sign‑up or KYC completion.
- Payment failures or high transaction decline rates.
- Rising fraud losses or false‑positive blocks.
- Unprofitable customer segments despite growth.

## 3. Common KPIs and metrics
- Activation rate, WAU/MAU, retention cohorts.
- Transaction success rate, payment conversion.
- Take rate, LTV/CAC, contribution margin.
- Fraud loss rate, chargeback rate.

## 4. Typical data sources
- Product analytics (event logs, funnels).
- Payment processor logs and reconciliation files.
- CRM and customer support/ticketing data.
- Risk and fraud monitoring systems.

## 5. How this domain uses the standard BA workflow
- **Problem framing:** define growth, payment, or risk objective.
- **Data collection:** combine product events, payments, and risk data.
- **Cleaning:** align event schemas, deduplicate users, reconcile transactions.
- **Analysis:** funnel, cohort, and unit‑economics analysis.
- **Insights:** quantify impact of friction points and pricing levers.
- **Communication:** share roadmap priorities with product and risk teams.
