# EdTech Domain – Business Analyst Guide

## 1. What a Business Analyst typically works on in this domain
- Learner acquisition, activation, and course completion rates.
- Content performance and curriculum effectiveness.
- Subscription or cohort‑based revenue performance.
- Engagement across web/mobile learning platforms.

## 2. Common business problems
- Low course completion or drop‑off after onboarding.
- Weak conversion from free trials to paid plans.
- High churn in subscription‑based products.
- Ineffective content or instructor performance.

## 3. Common KPIs and metrics
- Course completion rate, lesson engagement rate.
- Trial‑to‑paid conversion, churn rate.
- Monthly active learners, time‑on‑platform.
- NPS, learner satisfaction scores.

## 4. Typical data sources
- Learning management systems (LMS) and event logs.
- Subscription/billing platforms.
- CRM and marketing automation tools.
- Feedback surveys and support tickets.

## 5. How this domain uses the standard BA workflow
- **Problem framing:** define learner success or growth objective.
- **Data collection:** pull LMS events, subscription, and feedback data.
- **Cleaning:** normalize course IDs, handle incomplete sessions.
- **Analysis:** funnel to completion, cohort retention, content impact.
- **Insights:** identify drivers of engagement and revenue growth.
- **Communication:** share recommendations with product and content teams.
