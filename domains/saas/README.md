# SaaS Domain – Business Analyst Guide

## 1. What a Business Analyst typically works on in this domain
- Product usage analytics and customer health scoring.
- Subscription lifecycle (trial → paid → renewal → expansion).
- Pricing and packaging optimization.
- Support performance and customer success outcomes.

## 2. Common business problems
- Low trial‑to‑paid conversion.
- Rising churn in specific customer cohorts.
- Under‑utilized features or low product adoption.
- Expansion revenue below targets.

## 3. Common KPIs and metrics
- Trial conversion rate, activation rate.
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR), churn rate.
- MRR/ARR, expansion rate.
- Feature adoption rate, support resolution time.

## 4. Typical data sources
- Product analytics and event logs.
- Billing and subscription management systems.
- CRM and customer success platforms.
- Support ticketing tools.

## 5. How this domain uses the standard BA workflow
- **Problem framing:** define churn, conversion, or adoption objective.
- **Data collection:** consolidate product events and subscription data.
- **Cleaning:** align account IDs, normalize plan tiers, handle upgrades.
- **Analysis:** cohort retention, usage‑to‑renewal correlation.
- **Insights:** identify key drivers of retention and expansion.
- **Communication:** deliver playbooks for product, CS, and sales teams.
