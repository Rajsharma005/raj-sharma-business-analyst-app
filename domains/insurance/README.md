# Insurance Domain – Business Analyst Guide

## 1. What a Business Analyst typically works on in this domain
- Policy lifecycle analytics (quote → bind → renew).
- Claims processing efficiency and loss ratio management.
- Customer retention and cross‑sell across product lines.
- Regulatory and compliance reporting needs.

## 2. Common business problems
- Low quote‑to‑bind conversion rates.
- High claims costs or slow claims turnaround.
- Lapses at renewal or poor retention in key segments.
- Inconsistent underwriting outcomes.

## 3. Common KPIs and metrics
- Quote conversion rate, bind rate.
- Loss ratio, combined ratio.
- Claims cycle time, claim approval rate.
- Renewal rate, persistency.

## 4. Typical data sources
- Policy administration systems.
- Claims management platforms.
- Underwriting and risk assessment tools.
- Customer service and broker/agent systems.

## 5. How this domain uses the standard BA workflow
- **Problem framing:** define conversion, claims, or retention goals.
- **Data collection:** pull policy, claims, and underwriting data.
- **Cleaning:** standardize policy IDs, coverage types, and claim statuses.
- **Analysis:** conversion funnel, loss ratio by segment, claims aging.
- **Insights:** identify levers to improve underwriting and claims efficiency.
- **Communication:** provide recommendations for pricing, risk, and ops teams.
