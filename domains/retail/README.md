# Retail Domain – Business Analyst Guide

## 1. What a Business Analyst typically works on in this domain
- Store performance and merchandising effectiveness.
- Inventory planning, replenishment, and shrink analysis.
- Customer loyalty and basket‑building initiatives.
- Promotions and pricing impact analysis.

## 2. Common business problems
- Stockouts on high‑velocity items.
- Low conversion or declining basket size.
- High shrinkage or markdowns.
- Ineffective promotional campaigns.

## 3. Common KPIs and metrics
- Sales per store, sales per square foot.
- Basket size, units per transaction.
- Inventory turnover, shrink rate.
- Promo lift, gross margin.

## 4. Typical data sources
- POS systems and store transactions.
- Inventory and replenishment systems.
- Loyalty program data.
- Promotion and pricing systems.

## 5. How this domain uses the standard BA workflow
- **Problem framing:** define store or category performance objectives.
- **Data collection:** extract POS, inventory, and promo datasets.
- **Cleaning:** reconcile store IDs, handle returns/exchanges.
- **Analysis:** category performance, promo effectiveness, basket analysis.
- **Insights:** quantify margin impact and replenishment improvements.
- **Communication:** deliver recommendations to merchandising and ops teams.
