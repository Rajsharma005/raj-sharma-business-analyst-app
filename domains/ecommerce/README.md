# E-commerce Domain – Business Analyst Guide

## 1. What a Business Analyst typically works on in this domain
- Customer acquisition and conversion optimization.
- Merchandising performance and category profitability.
- Fulfillment, delivery, and returns analysis.
- Pricing, promotions, and campaign effectiveness.

## 2. Common business problems
- Cart abandonment and low checkout conversion.
- High return rates impacting margin.
- Inventory stockouts or overstock issues.
- Inefficient marketing spend or low ROAS.

## 3. Common KPIs and metrics
- Conversion rate, cart abandonment rate.
- AOV (Average Order Value), gross margin.
- Return rate, on‑time delivery rate.
- ROAS, CAC, repeat purchase rate.

## 4. Typical data sources
- Web/app analytics and event tracking.
- Order management and inventory systems.
- Marketing platforms and ad spend data.
- Customer support and returns systems.

## 5. How this domain uses the standard BA workflow
- **Problem framing:** define conversion or fulfillment goals and constraints.
- **Data collection:** extract traffic, order, inventory, and campaign data.
- **Cleaning:** reconcile order IDs, handle partial shipments/returns.
- **Analysis:** funnel analysis, category profitability, promo lift.
- **Insights:** quantify impact of UX or pricing changes.
- **Communication:** share recommendations with growth and ops teams.
